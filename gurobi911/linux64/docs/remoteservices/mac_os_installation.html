Mac OS Installation

On Mac OS, your next step once you've downloaded the Gurobi Remote Services package from our website (e.g., gurobi_server9.1.0_mac64.pkg for Gurobi 9.1.0) is to double-click on the installer and follow the prompts. By default, the installer will place the Gurobi Remote Services 9.1.0 files in /Library/gurobi_server910/mac64 (note that this is the system /Library directory, not your personal  /Library directory). Your <installdir> (which we'll refer to throughout this document) will be /Library/gurobi_server910/mac64.
